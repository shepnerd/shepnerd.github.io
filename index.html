Biography

I am currently a 2nd year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia. My research interests include computer vision and deep learning. Recently I am working on image generation.

Before that, I got the M.Sc. and B.Eng. degree from Peking University (supervised by Prof. Yuexian Zou) and Northwest A&F University (supervised by Prof. Cheng Cai), respectively.
